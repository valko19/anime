jujutsu kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen, a shounen anime that started in 2020, with intense battles and supernatural themes, is ongoing in Japanese

Written by: Gege Akutami

Cast: Junya Enoki, Yuichi Nakamura, Yuma Uchida

The plot of Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of curses after consuming a powerful cursed object. Joining the Tokyo Jujutsu High, he teams up with other sorcerers to fight dangerous curses while harboring the curse of Sukuna, one of the most malevolent entities. The story combines supernatural battles, friendship, and the protagonist's quest to control the evil power within him.
